Break The Loots

This extension simplifies your spam on Loots website. You just need to check the Google Recaptcha and the extension sends your loots.

Available variables:

  • {count}: Streamer's counter. 1- character(s)
  • {date}: Current date. character(s)
  • {time}: Current time. character(s)
  • {datetime}: Current date and time. character(s)

Twitch Follows

You can configure a message per streamer. You just need to enter your nickname and save or synchronize data to update the follows list.
